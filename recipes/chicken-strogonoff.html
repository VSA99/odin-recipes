Chicken Strogonoff

Description

The chicken strogonoff is a dish loved by all, and it's really easy to make. The thought of the juicy chicken meat combined with creamy sauce leaves everyone salivating. The original strogonoff comes from Russia and the brasilian version has a few differences. The brasilian can be done with beef, chicken or shrimp, the sauce is thicker, made with cream, and tomato sauce or ketchup to give color and flavor to the sauce. In addition, the brasilian version is served with straw potatoes, to give it crunchiness, or with French fries.

Ingredients

  • Chicken in cubes.
  • Onion and garlic.
  • Tomato products.
  • Butter.
  • Spices and seasonings.
  • Mustard.
  • Ketchup.
  • Cream.

Steps

  1. In a pan mix the chicken the onion and garlic.
  2. Cook the chicken.
  3. Add the ketchup and mustard.
  4. Add the cream and take it out of the fire before boiling.
  5. Serve with rice or straw potatoes
