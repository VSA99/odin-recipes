Best Homemade Lemonade Ever

Description

This lemonade recipe makes a very refreshing drink!

Ingredients

  • Sugar.
  • Water.
  • Lemon, juiced.
  • Ice.

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and water to make a syrup, by boiling the water with the sugar.
  2. Pour the syrup into a pitcher and mix it with the lemon juice and stir it.
  3. Add the ice.
  4. Serve!
